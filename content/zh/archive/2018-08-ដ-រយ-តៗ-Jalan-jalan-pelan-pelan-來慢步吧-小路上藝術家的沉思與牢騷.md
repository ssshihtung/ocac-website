►活動簡介  :   

此次短暫在打開-當代藝術工作站駐留的期間，藝術節策展人Sinta和藝術家Vatey將與大家一起討論他們在藝術領域工作時不時需要面對的課題及種種與生活分不開的難題。

例如：

°°假如時間不再是金錢，龜速是否能讓我們學到些什麼°°

°°為什麼到哪都要一直做發表咧？我們的出現可以只當個特別的禮物嗎？°°

°°比起掙扎按部就班以求達成目標，依著自己的步調走是不是個比較平靜的選擇？°°

°°如何能無痛擺脫我們的習慣°°

°°行動如何改變心靈的能量°°

在一次名為『互不相識（Mutual Unknown）』”的展覽中，Vatey和Sinta遇到了幾位來自東南亞的藝術家及策展人，他們立即接上了線，並開始探討「（地理上的）詩性  (geo)poetics 」”如何作為顛覆「（地理上的）政治性（geo）politics」的方法。期待探索更多的未知，他們發起了『觀看 & 漫遊/驚奇 走路工作坊』(watch＆wa / onder walkshops)和『重制回憶（re-enacting memories）』的展覽。 

快來OCAC與他們面對面，並探索藝術的無限可能，這可能讓我們失去理智！！！

結束後，我想我們也會餓到不行，歡迎每個飢腸轆轆的人和我們一起共進晚餐！

►講者簡介  :

TAN Vatey

TAN Vatey 生於1992年，柬埔寨金邊。是視覺藝術創作者，她的作品以繪畫及複合媒材裝置為主，並熱衷於探索不同的藝術形式。她對當代藝術的興趣始於某次她在“莎莎藝術計畫（Sa Sa Art Projects）“所上的課程。她曾於柬埔寨金邊的SA SA BASSAC、比利時的NEWFOUNDLAND及泰國清邁的e x t a n t a t i o n駐村。作品曾於瑞典駐金邊大使館、荷蘭布魯塞爾等地展出。近期的計畫則是在中國南方的沙溪鎮研究農民社群。

Sinta Wibowo
 
Sinta Wibowo生於1978年，比利時魯汶。生活工作於荷蘭布魯塞爾及柬埔寨金邊，並遍及歐洲、前蘇聯國家及亞洲的社群網絡。父母為70年代從印尼移民至比利時的印尼華人。在研習經濟學與市場行銷時，她參與與表演藝術、電影、視覺藝術及其他相關領域的計畫，目前擔任藝術節製作人，文化策略和顧問。

► About the Event :   

In residency at OCAC, Sinta & Vatey will share their common journey in the field of arts that crosses paths with life-related questions such as:

°° What if time is no longer money, can turtle time teach us something°°

°° Why the constant need to present, can we just be present°°

°° Is pace-orientation a more peaceful way than the struggle between goal versus process-orientation°°

°° How to happily unhabit our habits°°

°° How can movement cause mobility of the mind°°

Bumping into each other during the "Mutual Unknown" exhibition with several artists & curators from South-East Asia, Vatey and Sinta connected instantly and together they started to explore (geo)poetics as a way to subvert (geo)politics. Looking up more of the unknown, they initated "watch & wa/onder walkshops" and [re-enacting memories] live exhibitions. Come meet them at OCAC and let's multiply meanings of art that can meander out of our minds!

Afterwards, we'll surely be hungry and happy to have dinner outside with those who are hungry too! 

►About speakers：

TAN Vatey

TAN Vatey (1992, Phnom Penh) is a visual artist who creates drawings, paintings and mixed-media art installations. She is keen in exploring different art forms and currently experimenting with sound and moving images.

Her interest in contemporary art started with a class at Sa Sa Art Projects in 2016, after which, during a residency at SA SA BASSAC, she created [premature cultures], displaying strings of vegetables slowly suffocating in plastic bags, hanging from the ceiling. This is currently part of the group exhibition “Facing the Climate” touring throughout Cambodia, organized by the Embassy of Sweden in Phnom Penh.

She created [masshgrooming] as part of the CuratorsLab exhibition "Mutual Unknown" at the National Gallery of Indonesia, exploring health issues and mass consumption in Indonesian culture, exhibiting a field of mushrooms made from kretek cigarettes, instant noodles and krupuk (shrimp chips). 

Vatey also had a solo-exhibition called [re-enacting memories] with Sinta Widowo in the frame of Europalia Indonesia in Brussels. Her latest creation [power ranges] was presented at Sangker Gallery in Battambang. Vatey did residencies at NEWFOUNDLAND in Mechelen, Belgium at e x t a n t a t i o n in Chiangmai, Thailand and was recently researching farmers' communities in Shaxi, South-China.

website: http://vateytan.wixsite.com/artist

Sinta Wibowo
 
Sinta Wibowo (1978, Leuven) has her basis in both Brussels & Phnom Penh, with her Indonesian-Chinese parents migrating from Indonesia to Belgium in the 70’s. While studying economics &  marketing, Sinta connected to a continuation of projects, festivals and art institutions in the performing arts, film, visual arts and other fields, as a festival maker, cultural strategist & consultant. 

She feels well working closely with others like artists, institutions, curators as a caretaker who engages to the realization of their programs and ideas. In these collaborations, Sinta tends to work cross-disciplinary, transsectorially, intra-regionally & transnationally, stimulates experiment in research, mobilizes discourse & methodology, aspires to give more body to diversity, inclusion & outreach, encourages collaborations & partnerships and follows (cultural) policies on local, regional national, & international level. Being involved in different networks, she shifts between centre & periphery, migrating within East-West-Europe, Post-Soviet countries & Asia and enjoys stimulating exchange of experience, knowledge & personal contacts. 

A turning point in Sinta's work and life was the Sideways Walking Arts Festival, where everyone is invited for a stroll and explore ‘pathways’ on foot, as part of an itinerant artistic laboratory. After crossing paths with TAN Vatey in 2017, another dimension of exploration opened with [re-enacting memories] and watch & wa/onder, two avenues where they experiment with the notions of time, space & self in all their relations while creating spaces of togetherness & trust.
