在技術、道德及相關宇宙論的交匯處，不斷循環、提問，所產生的結果是什麼？兩年間，這些行動積累了什麼樣的反饋，從而引發對社會場域的重新想像，打開對溝通的多元見解和對未來的思考。古今智慧和歷代網路流動於當代景觀中，我們是否期望培養一種可持續的身心重新定向？在全球同步的局面下找尋隙縫間通往新本體論的路徑，並維持心中的宇宙圖像以支撐對養身的需求？過去兩年中「未來道」持續展開對道的實驗性詮釋，並在其中思考技術、存在和認知的關係。在當前階段重新評估表演性的社會邏輯，並思考我們面對的提問對象。

lololol 共同創辦人林亭君和張欣延續發展「未來道」計畫，分別以《3C形意拳》和《小青龍湯》兩個系列創作建構兩種截然不同的實踐方向。透過lololol在英國貝里克電影媒體藝術節與藝術家喬治 · 克拉克（George Clark）和泛靈影像計劃Animistic Apparatus的合作經驗，尋找存在和不存在的邊界相連，並借鏡智利電影導演拉烏 ‧ 盧伊茲（Raul Ruiz）未完成的電影《影子喜劇》其中一句台詞，「不能說開機，要說金、木、水、火、土」，試圖開啟一種對表演行動的重新定位。

關於 lololol
「lololol」是循環的笑聲，0與1構成無盡陰陽變化，同時演示電腦語言的基礎脈動。lololol時而喜出望外、暗自竊喜、或喜怒不形於色。lololol 悠遊於虛實之間的地平線上，不受限於制定的文化邏輯或角色扮演，時而發出的滄海一聲笑。

Future Tao: “Action”

Location｜OCAC (Open Contemporary Art Center)
                  No.25, Ganzhou St., Datong Dist., Taipei City 103, Taiwan
Date | 2019 Nov 15th to 2019 Dec 15th
           Wed. to Sun. 14:00-20:00, Night projection 20:00-02:00
Opening｜Nov 24th (Sun) 14:00-20:00

What is being generated in a collective practice that continually performs questions within the intersections of technologies, morality and their underlying cosmological frameworks? What kind of feedback do these actions accumulate, that spark re-imaginations of sociopheres, open alternative methods of communication and ideas of the future? Do we attempt to sustain a reorientation of our bodies and minds in a landscape that is flowing between new and old realms of intelligence and networks? To renew ontological grounds sprouting between the dominating surface of universal synchronicity? How do we sustain mental cosmographies that provide sustenance for different modes of wellbeing? For the last two years, Future Tao has been a processing of experimental conceptions of Tao in relation to technology, cognition and phenomenologies. In the current phase, we are reassessing the nature of performative action and to whom our questions are addressed.

lololol founders Xia Lin and Sheryl Cheung continue to expand upon the premise of Future Tao and present “3C Xing Yi Quan” and “Little Dragon Soup,” two distinct projects with shared interest grounds in ontological explorations. The show builds upon their recent collaboration with artist George Clark and cinematic project Animistic Apparatus at Berwick Film & Media Arts Festival this summer. During their collaboration, a particular quote from The Comedy of Shadows by Chiliean film director Raul Ruiz resonated as a proposal for redirection, “Do not say action. Say metal, wood, water, fire, earth.1” 

ABOUT lololol
lololol is a boundless laughter, an endless extension of lol (laugh out loud), an acronym that appears to be constructed by the building blocks of I-Ching and/or computer code. lololol is a hearty release amidst interchanging states of existing and non-existing, emptiness and substance, industrial and mystical, without resistance or submission to any singular logic or assumption, to respond to the world with an evolving vibration that spreads across the seas.
