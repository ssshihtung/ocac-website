## **WE ARE LOCATED AT**

No.25, Ganzhou St., Datong Dist., Taipei City 103, Taiwan (R.O.C.)

**OPENING HOURS**

Wednesday to Sunday, 2pm-8pm

close to Monday and Tuseday.

**TRAFFIC INFOMATION**

**BY MRT**

Take the metro to Daqiaotou station, exit the station from Exit NO. 2, Straight the Sec. 2, Chongqing S. Rd, walk towards starbucks, turn right, first street turn left to Ganzhou street, It is a 5 seconds walk to OCAC from the street entrance.

##  **BY BUS**

Get off the intersection of Chongqing S. Rd and Liangzhou St, '' walk towards starbucks turn left to Ganzhou street'', It is a 5 seconds walk to OCAC from the street entrance.

The following buses stop at the '' intersection of Chongqing S. Rd and Liangzhou St '' 2, 63, 215, 223, 250, 288, 302, 306, 601, 636, 639, 757, 892, 893, 1209, 1210, 1501, 1505, 1811, 1812, 1816, 1818, 1822, 1823, 1824, 9002, 9003.

**BY CAR**

Sec. 2, Chongqing S. Rd turn right to Guisui St, first street is Ganzhou street, There are a small park in the Guisui St, OCAC is in a one-way street, Please park the car legally, and it is a one minutes walk to OCAC from the Guisui St.
