## Visiting Information

### Address
No. 20, Lane 43, Nanmen Road, West Central District, Tainan

### Opening Hours
Open is an artist-run, independently operated space with flexible opening hours. We recommend checking our Facebook page for current visiting hours and upcoming activities before your visit to ensure we're open.

### How to Get Here
Open is conveniently located in Tainan's city center, near the Tainan City Government and the Confucius Temple. The space is easily accessible by public bus or car from Tainan Railway Station and is within a short distance of downtown Tainan.

We warmly welcome all visitors interested in contemporary art. We look forward to seeing you at our open art space!
