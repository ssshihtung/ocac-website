Wan Shu-Yun was born in Taichung, Taiwan and earned Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology from National Chung Cheng University, Taiwan. She is currently studying Fine Arts in Taipei National University of the Arts in Taiwan. She constantly considers the relationship between individual, time and space in her artistic practice, reviewing studies of Human History, Anthropology and Psychology, using the idea of “Drawing” to capture the outline of the connection between human beings and the world. She extends her inner impulse of drawing toward painting, writing, collecting, recording, collage, action, installation, etc. Others from independent practice, she is also an active member of the art group “Duckly Reading Group”, which using writing as a starter to explore the possible interaction between words, visual art and theatre.
