Hoo Fan-Chon (b. 1982) is a visual artist / curator based in George Town, Penang, Malaysia. He completed his BA photography at the London College of Communication and has shown his work in local and international exhibitions. He is one of the founding members of an art collective - Run Amok Gallery based in George Town, Penang. He is currently participating in a curatorial programme titled Southeast Asian Curators’ Woskshop 2016/17 , organised by the Japan Foundation.
