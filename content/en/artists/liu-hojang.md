Hojang Liu’s photographic works and projects concerns more than image making or technology quality. His peculiar artistic practice often reflects the social context and politics behind the surface—may it be an object, a place, or a community, his works shed light on their individualities and differences. By appropriating the relation between the visible and the invisible, he provides an authentic narratives to the social functions and sensibility of art to transform the values of human network into forces that sustains art in its becoming.
