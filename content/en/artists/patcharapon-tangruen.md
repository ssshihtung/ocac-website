Patcharapon Tangruen was born 1981 in Thailand; also known as Alex Face for his graffiti art. Roaming in the dark corners of the urban environment, he presented these inspirations from street life in art galleries. Many his work features the character of a girl child in rabbit costume. Alex received a BFA from King Mongkut’s Institute of Technology Ladkrabang, Thailand. He has exhibited in many international Street Art Festivals, including Moniker Art Fair, London, UK (2013); Exhibition 20th Anniversary Department of Fine Art, KMITL, Thailand (2013); BUK RUK Street Art Festival, Bangkok Thailand (2013); “*The Rendezvous*”, South East Asia urban art event, Yangon, Myanmar (2012), etc. He also had solo exhibitions, such as “*KA-MOAD 2013*”, Mite Ugro ZaZa Gallery, Gwang Ju, South Korea (2013); “*The Underground Adventure*”, Artery postmodern gallery, Bangkok (2012); and “*After the Quake*”, Chiang Mai, Thailand (2010).
