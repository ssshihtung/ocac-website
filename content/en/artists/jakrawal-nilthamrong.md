Born 1977. Jakrawal got an MFA in Art and Technology from the Art Institute of Chicago. From 2009 until now he has been a lecturer on Film Subject at the Faculty of Journalism and Mass Communication, Thammasat University. In 2009 he received the Hubert Bals Fund for his script development course. In 2008 he attended the Berlinale Talent Campus. Besides his work as a director, in 2010, with his installation/film project 'Unreal Forest' at Numthong Gallery,Thailand. The project was -among other places - screened/exhibited at CPH:DOX FF last year, Rotterdam Int FF and Göteborg Int. FF.
