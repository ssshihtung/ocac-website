Jekky lives and works in Bangkok. He graduated from the Faculty of Painting Sculpture and Graphic Arts in Silpakorn University. He used to be a creative director in J. Walter Thomson advertising agency, his works were awarded many times. He also is a guest lecturer in Advertising and Creative classes at many leading universities. In 2012, his first solo show “*Stuff like That*” was exhibited in Tao Hong Tai / d Kunst Gallery in Ratchaburi, Thailand; he was also part of “*THAITAI : A Measure of Understanding*”, a contemporary art exchange between Taiwan and Thailand, in Bangkok Art and Culture Centre (2012); and “*Passion **Vicinity*” at the Metropolitan hotel, Bangkok (2011); and “*Rakuza*”, in a pottery factory Tao Hong Tai, in Rachaburi. He has recently curated street art actions in Rachaburi called “*Tid Silp bon Ratchaburi 2 : **We are the City*”.
