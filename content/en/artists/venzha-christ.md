Based in Yogyakarta, Indonesia.
Was ever invited to Asian Art Biennial in 2013 by National Taiwan Museum of Fine Arts, Venzha Christ’s personal works focus on new media art. He determined to fuse education, art and technology with local communities without cultural limitation, to reinforce the interest and analysis of technology in artistic production and theory. He established many cross-disciplined labs, festivals, research platforms and workshops in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. Among these, the most famous is “HONF Foundation” since1999, and v.u.f.o.c: an extraterrestrial study center. His team presented media art projects in many places and countries worldwide. He also brought the first “FabLab” to South East Asia.
