‘Un/Uttered — Asian Art Collectives in Dialogue & Micro Film Festival’ was co-hosted by OCAC, Tra-Travel (Japan) and Osaka Art Hub at FIGYA, Osaka, in February 2024. Programmed by P.M.S. with participants including Kawah Umei, Rngrang Hungul and Posak Jodian, the screenings featured works by Taiwanese indigenous directors: ‘My Days on Linsen North Road’, ‘I Am a Woman, I Am a Hunter’, ‘Mother Tongue’, ‘Ugaljai – Moth’ and ‘Misafafahiyan – Metamorphosis’.
