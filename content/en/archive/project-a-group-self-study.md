‘A Group Self-Study’ is a self-organised community of curators and curiosity-led friends who have been gathering at OCAC every last-Sunday evening since 2016. After finishing the Chinese translation of *Cultures of the Curatorial* in summer 2019, they launched the ‘Artist–Curator Mutual Aid Meeting’, folding artistic practitioners into their self-study process.
