From 2010 to early 2011, Chuang Che-Wei has been exhibited in many important international shows for the first time overseas, including December 2010 “Taiwan Calling” organised by NTMoFA in Hungary, and “Taiwan Contemporary Art”, opened in 2011 in Italy. The artwork seemed to be creating a specific space with drawings; yet it is about how to describe visually “fear” and “desire” with the content of drawings. Thus there are many narrative image groups repeated in the fragmented drawings.
