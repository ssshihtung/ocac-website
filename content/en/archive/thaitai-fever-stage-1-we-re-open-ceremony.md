*TAHITI FEVER* project is hosted in OCAC Bangkok space. In August 2012, it launched the first exhibition *Stage 1: WE’re OPEN*! It was a Thai style ceremony for moving into a house, collaboratively ran by members of OCAC and Thai artist Jiandyin (Jiradej and Pornpilai Meemalai). After the 2-hour ceremony, the recorded sound, objects and portfolio from members of OCAC was combined as installation artwork in the exhibition.
